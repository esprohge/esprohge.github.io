Star Wars Survey Project¶

The goal of this project is to analyze data from a FiveThirtyEight surevy of Star Wars fans. In particular, we want to address the question of whether fans recognize the film "The Empire Strikes Back" as the best of the series.

The first two graphs above indicate that fans of the series ranked the original triad of films as much better than the later triad. Non-fans still ranked Episode V as the best, but ranked the later triad more closely to the original triad. In fact, they ranked Episode I nearly evenly with Episode V. In regard to viewership, fans generally have seen all six films, whereas non-fans have seen Episodes V and VI, and to a lesser extent Episode I, much more than Episodes II-IV.
